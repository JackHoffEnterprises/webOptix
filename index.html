About us

What is Optix?

Optix is a website driven to offer trading view indicators for specified commodities, stocks, and cryptos as well as a community of traders sharing their historical experience while growing together. Optix uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to adjust parameters for the sets of indicators within the various packages. After several steps of optimization, a proprietary index calculation is chosen by the AI then applied within the indicator package of your choice.

What is a Strategy?

A trading strategy is an established method of planning and making trades. Trading strategies typically set out specifications for which trades to make, when to make them, when to exit them, and how much capital you should risk on each position.

What is an Indicator?

Trading indicators are mathematical computations plotted as lines on a price chart that aid traders in identifying certain signs and trends in the stock market. They are simply a set of tools applied to a trading chart to demystify the market and make a clearer analysis.

What is an Optimizer ?

Optimization is the process of making a trading system more effective by adjusting the variables used for technical analysis. A trading system can be optimized by reducing certain transaction costs or risks, or by targeting assets with greater expected returns. (The optimizer loads strategies into a python based terminal then it uses a series of indicators, and a weighting factor for each indicator, to determine potential trade signals). Signals can then be used at the sole discretion of the end user.

How Does Optix Utilize Artificial Intelligence?

We have taken the path to make our strategies “smarter” overtime by implementing a form of “AI” referred to as machine learning during our strategy optimization process. Here we start running possible outcomes within a set of parameters, hereinafter referred to as iterations. Once an iteration is complete, a new iteration is run centered around the best solution from the previous iteration and using a smaller step size; overtime reaching a proposed perfect solution.

Do These Packages Automate My Trades

No, these packages do not automate your trades. The packages will give you access to Invite Only TradingView Indicators that have undergone our proprietary optimization and parameter setting processes. We encourage you to purchase our educational package to learn more about various ways to get started!

Does Optix Trade On My Behalf?

No, we do not handle user funds in any scenario. Optix provides Indicator packages that are to be used at the sole discretion of the end user. We do not guarantee performance metrics and market sentiments can change at any time. Use at your own risk.
